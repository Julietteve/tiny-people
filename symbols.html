    Symbol Meaning
    Frösö Runestone

    This northern-most raised runestone in Scandinavia, represents the furthest place we know before meeting the unknown.
    The Sun

    Represents the completion of the Great Work, life , influence and strength. Its importance lays on carrying something of value between two Worlds.
    The three realities

    The saami shamanic drum and the representation of the three layers of reality. The Upper world (Ylinen), the realm of gods and light spirits. The Middle World (Keskinen), spirit side of this mundane world. The Lower World (Alinen), realm of power animals, ancestral spirits, gods of the dead people
    The bird

    Mutation, elevation. Trespassing from one state to another. Becoming the opposite of one itself
    The drum

    The join of Air, Water, Fire and Earth in just one place
